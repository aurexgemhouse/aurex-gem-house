Colour & Quality Guide

Colour

Vivid, saturated green with slight bluish undertones is most desirable. Colombian emeralds display warmer green; Zambian emeralds show cooler tones.

Clarity

Inclusions ("jardin") are expected and accepted in emeralds. Eye-clean emeralds are exceptionally rare and command significant premiums.

Treatment

Minor oil treatment is standard and accepted. Cedar oil or colorless oil enhancement is industry norm. Heavily treated stones reduce value significantly.

Cultural & Traditional Significance

  • Birthstone: May

Emerald is highly valued in Chinese culture for its auspicious green colour, representing growth, harmony, and financial success.

Investment & Market Appeal in Australia

Emeralds have shown strong appreciation in the Australian market, particularly fine Colombian material. As supply diminishes from historic Colombian mines, investment-grade emeralds continue to appreciate. Australian-Chinese buyers particularly value emeralds for their cultural significance and wealth symbolism. Certification from prestigious laboratories ensures authenticity and value retention.
