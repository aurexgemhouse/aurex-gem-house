Colour & Quality Guide

Colour

"Pigeon blood" red with slight purple undertones is most prized. Vivid, saturated red without brown modifiers commands premium prices.

Clarity

Minor inclusions are acceptable in rubies‚Äîperfectly clean rubies are exceedingly rare. Silk inclusions indicate natural formation.

Treatment

Heat treatment is standard and accepted industry practice. Unheated "no-heat" rubies command significant premiums.

Rubies have demonstrated exceptional appreciation in the Australian and global markets. Fine pigeon-blood rubies from Myanmar command record prices at auction.
