Astrological Gemstones by Zodiac Sign

Astrological gemstones have been treasured for centuries for their ability to align personal energy with the zodiac. At Aurex Gem House, we offer ethically sourced, certified gemstones selected for their beauty, rarity, and astrological significance. Discover the gemstone that resonates with your zodiac sign.

  • Aries (March 21 – April 19) — Diamond

    Diamond symbolizes strength, courage, and clarity. It is believed to enhance confidence, leadership, and success, helping Aries stay focused and resilient in all pursuits.

  • Taurus (April 20 – May 20) — Emerald

    Emerald represents wisdom, harmony, and growth. It is believed to promote emotional balance, prosperity, and clear communication, supporting Taurus in both personal and professional life.

  • Gemini (May 21 – June 20) — Pearl

    Pearl symbolizes purity, calmness, and emotional balance. It is believed to soothe the mind, enhance communication, and bring inner peace to the curious and expressive Gemini.

  • Cancer (June 21 – July 22) — Ruby

    Ruby represents vitality, passion, and protection. It is believed to strengthen emotional security, boost confidence, and attract love and positive energy.

  • Leo (July 23 – August 22) — Peridot

    Peridot symbolizes joy, strength, and renewal. It is believed to attract abundance, reduce stress, and support Leo’s natural leadership and confidence.

  • Virgo (August 23 – September 22) — Sapphire

    Sapphire represents wisdom, clarity, and discipline. It is believed to enhance focus, intuition, and mental peace, helping Virgo stay balanced and goal-oriented.

  • Libra (September 23 – October 22) — Opal

    Opal symbolizes creativity, harmony, and emotional expression. It is believed to inspire love, balance relationships, and enhance Libra’s natural charm and elegance.

  • Scorpio (October 23 – November 21) — Topaz

    Topaz represents strength, clarity, and transformation. It is believed to bring emotional stability, courage, and protection while attracting success and good fortune.

  • Sagittarius (November 22 – December 21) — Tanzanite

    Tanzanite symbolizes insight, transformation, and spiritual growth. It is believed to enhance wisdom, creativity, and higher awareness for adventurous Sagittarius.

  • Capricorn (December 22 – January 19) — Garnet

    Garnet represents stability, protection, and ambition. It is believed to boost confidence, determination, and success, supporting Capricorn’s disciplined nature.

  • Aquarius (January 20 – February 18) — Amethyst

    Amethyst symbolizes peace, intuition, and spiritual clarity. It is believed to calm the mind, enhance creativity, and promote emotional balance.

  • Pisces (February 19 – March 20) — Aquamarine

    Aquamarine represents serenity, courage, and clarity. It is believed to soothe emotions, strengthen intuition, and promote gentle confidence.
